Shelley Mannor

Third person puzzle game, with fixed cameras in the style of the old resident evil games, made in UE 5.2


Find your way through a puzzle-filled manor while barely dodging human eating monsters.
You wake up in a dark room with one main objective: escape those sharpened teeth.
However you shall not worry, you will meet friends along the way.

Available on Steam
