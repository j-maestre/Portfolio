Cloth & Rope simulations

Cloth & Ropes physics simulation using C++

This simulation uses Verlet Integration to calculate the smooth and stable movements of the particles

that make up the cloth and strings.

In addition, it applies Jakobsen's Constraint Solver to maintain the distances between particles,
achieving a realistic and coherent representation of the material.

This physics are executed in a paralel thread using a multhithread system
